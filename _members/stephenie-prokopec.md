Stephenie Prokopec is a Bioinformatician at the Centre for Discovery in Cancer Research (CDCR) at McMaster University. Stephenie has over 15 years of experience working with cancer genomics, working previously at Princess Margeret Cancer Center and the Ontario Institute for Cancer Research. Stephenie is passionate about applying computational tools to advance our understanding of cancer biology.
